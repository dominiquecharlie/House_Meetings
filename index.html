What is this?

This is our shared space to reflect before we all sit down together. Take a few minutes here to share what's been great, what's worth a conversation, and what we're hoping for. Then we look at it all together.

How it works

Each of us fills this out on our own device, in our own time. When we're all done, we open the report together and let it guide the meeting. The AI will write a warm synthesis of all our responses, plus we'll see a side-by-side of what each person said.
